Predictors and Outcomes of Parental Burnout and Educational Involvement (POPP)

We conducted the POPP study between September 2023 and September 2024. The project was funded by the University of Luxembourg. A total of 1,373 U.S. parents of adolescents aged 12 to 15 participated in the study. POPP employed a four-wave panel design with three-month intervals between waves. In addition, an intensive longitudinal study was conducted with a subsample of participants between Time 3 and Time 4.
